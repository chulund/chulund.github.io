About Me

My Name is Nur Fajar Trihantoro. I am currently studying Master of Geospatial Science at Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology, Melbourne. I graduated from Geodesy and Geomatics Engineering at Institute of Technology Bandung in 2015. From 2015 - 2019, I worked in Geodesy Research Laboratory as a Reseach and Academic Assistant.

I excel in various GIS Software (ArcGIS, QGIS), AutoCAD, and Photogrammetry, as well as Remote Sensing Software. I am also a full-stack web developer and able to build a website using HTML, PHP, SQL, and JavaScript. I have developed several small to medium geospatial-related software in C++, MATLAB and python. I am familiar with DOS-based and UNIX-based operating system and able to act as a small scale system & network administrator. I have experience in training and teaching in surveying and geodetic equipment as well as surveying and GIS software. I have involved in several research projects about physical geodesy, GNSS positioning, and system instrumentation.

I am interested in research in several areas, including atmospheric science, physical geodesy, mathematical geodesy, WebGIS, geospatial big-data, and geodetic system and instrumentation. I plan to become a lecturer and researcher in the future. Therefore I am looking for an opportunity in research and education field currently.
