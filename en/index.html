Groom & Bride

Hi I am Nur Fajar. I was born and raised in Nongkojajar, a rural and beautiful town located at the Bromo Mountain valley. After finishing my school in Malang, I moved to Bandung in West Java to pursue my Geodesy and Geomatics degree at Institute of Technology Bandung (ITB). After graduating, I stayed in the university for a while to work as research assitant while applying for a master scholarship. I got an Australian Award Scholarship in 2018 to study Master of Geospatial Science in RMIT, Melbourne, Australia. I finished my study this mid-year and am currently back in Indonesia preparing for a PhD back in RMIT. I'm very passionate in teaching and researching. I'm planing to become a researcher in ITB after finishing my PhD.
